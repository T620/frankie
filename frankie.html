Toasts

I honestly hate the fact that these little notifications are called 'toasts', so I'm renaming them to something much less cringey in the future

Used to display notifications to the user, usually in real time. It's in the bottom right corner. They overlay content, similar to ctas. Eventually, I'll add a colour system so you can do things like .danger, and .success for red and green toasts, respectively.

Item added to basket!

Alerts

Next up, we have alerts. These are big toasts, meant for big threats or successess. They float and are persistent until the user dismisses them.

Again, eventually I'll add a colour system so you can do things like .danger, and .success for red and green toasts, respectively.
